# Ethics

This project is a **systematic review of published literature**. It does not involve human participants, personal data collection, or experimental interventions.

## What we do

- Search academic databases (PubMed, arXiv, Semantic Scholar) for published papers
- Screen papers against predefined inclusion/exclusion criteria
- Extract reported results from published studies
- Synthesize findings across studies

## AI-assisted screening

We use GPT-4o for title/abstract screening with a 3-model unanimous voting protocol. All AI decisions are logged and subject to human review. The AI assists with classification — it does not generate research claims.

## Academic integrity

- All sources are cited
- Results are reported as published by original authors
- Limitations of the review are disclosed
- No data is fabricated or falsified
