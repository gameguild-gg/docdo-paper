# Rebuttal Template

Structure for responding to reviewer feedback.

---

## Format

```markdown
# Rebuttal — [Paper Title]

**Venue:** [Conference/Journal]  
**Submission ID:** [if applicable]  
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Summary of Changes

[Brief overview of how we addressed the reviews]

---

## Response to Reviewer 1

### Concern 1: [Summary of concern]

> [Quote from review]

**Response:** [Our response]

**Changes made:** [Specific edits, with page/line numbers]

### Concern 2: [Summary of concern]

> [Quote from review]

**Response:** [Our response]

**Changes made:** [Specific edits]

---

## Response to Reviewer 2

[Same structure]

---

## Response to Reviewer 3

[Same structure]

---

## Additional Changes

[Any other improvements not directly requested]
```

---

## Rebuttal Guidelines

1. **Be respectful** — Thank reviewers for their time
2. **Be specific** — Point to exact changes (page, line)
3. **Be concise** — Address the concern directly
4. **Be complete** — Don't skip concerns
5. **Be honest** — If you disagree, explain why respectfully
