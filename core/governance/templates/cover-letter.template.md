# Cover Letter Template

Standard cover letter for journal submissions.

---

## Template

```markdown
[Date]

Dear Editor,

Please find attached our manuscript titled "[Title]" for consideration 
for publication in [Journal Name].

## Summary

[2-3 sentences summarizing the paper's contribution]

## Fit with Journal

[1-2 sentences explaining why this paper fits the journal's scope]

## Contribution

This paper makes the following contributions:

1. [Contribution 1]
2. [Contribution 2]
3. [Contribution 3]

## Declarations

- This manuscript has not been published elsewhere and is not under 
  consideration by another journal.
- All authors have approved the manuscript and agree to its submission.
- [If applicable] This research was approved by [ethics board].
- [If applicable] This research was funded by [funding source].

## Suggested Reviewers (if requested)

1. [Name], [Affiliation] — [email] — Expertise: [area]
2. [Name], [Affiliation] — [email] — Expertise: [area]

## Excluded Reviewers (if applicable)

[Names and brief justification]

Thank you for considering our work.

Sincerely,

[Author Name]
[Affiliation]
[Email]
```

---

## Notes

- Adjust formality based on venue conventions
- Some journals have specific cover letter requirements
- Check if suggested reviewers are required/optional
